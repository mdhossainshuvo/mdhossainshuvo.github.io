I am a tenure-track Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science at Prairie View A&M University. My research interests include:

> Computational Biology
> Machine Learning
> Data Analytics
> Artificial Intelligence

I am conducting interdisciplinary research at the intersection of structural biology, machine learning, and data science. Broadly, I am interested in developing computational methods for macromolecules to understand their structures and functions. My recent projects focus on harnessing the power of deep learning techniques, including geometric deep learning and protein language models, to understand the structural reliability of computationally generated protein molecules.

I am a recipient of the Pratt Fellowship Award, 2023 at Virginia Tech. I am also the recipient of the 1st place prize in the Young Scientist Excellence Award competition (MCBIOS, 2022) as well as multiple conference and travel fellowships.

Prior to joining PVAMU, I obtained my Ph.D. in Computer Science from Virginia Tech in 2023 under the supervision of Dr. Debswapna Bhattacharya.
